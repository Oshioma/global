What Guestlist is

Guestlist exists to surface experiences and spaces that are not designed for mass visibility. Many listings are private, time-sensitive, or intentionally unadvertised.

Who it is for

Hosts, curators, and participants who value discretion, quality, and context. Those comfortable operating without public validation.

How access works

Most access is referral-led. In some cases, aligned interest is noted and followed up privately. Silence should be considered a default outcome.

What access looks like

Limited invitations. Small groups. Private listings. Not everything is visible, and not everything repeats.

What this is not

Not a ticket marketplace, mailing list, or social feed. Guestlist does not optimise for reach or engagement metrics.

Conduct & discretion

Respect for hosts, spaces, and participants is assumed. Access is a privilege and may be withdrawn without explanation.
