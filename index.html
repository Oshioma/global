What Guestlist is

A private access layer for experiences and spaces that are not designed for mass visibility. Many listings are intentionally unadvertised, time-sensitive, or shared only through trusted circles.

How access works

Referral-led by default. Invitations are extended locally, city by city. In some cases, aligned interest is noted and followed up privately.
Silence should be considered the default outcome.

What access looks like

Private listings. Limited invitations. Small rooms.
Not everything is visible. Not everything repeats.

Who it is for

Hosts, curators, and participants who value discretion, quality, and context — and who can operate without public validation.
Guestlist is small on purpose.

What this is not

Not a ticket marketplace. Not a mailing list. Not a social feed. Guestlist does not optimise for engagement metrics.
This is not built for the crowd.

Conduct

Discretion is expected. Respect hosts and spaces. Access is a privilege and may be withdrawn without explanation.
