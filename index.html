What is Guestlist Global?

Guestlist Global is a private guestlist for humans choosing sovereignty: over their health, food, energy, water, education and community. We curate retreats, residencies, sound journeys and gatherings that support you to live more free – gently, steadily and with good people around you.

Sovereignty first
Every experience we list must support real sovereignty – from health and nervous system regulation to land, food and water security.
Sound, roots & frequency
We work with facilitators, musicians and spaces that understand frequency, intention and culture – not just “wellness trends”.
Pathways, not one-offs
Use Guestlist to test locations, build relationships, and slowly design your move away from the system, at a pace that fits your life.

SOUND • ROOTS • COMMUNITY

Sound, frequency and roots culture

Sound is one of the fastest ways to shift state – and one of the most ancient. Guestlist Global weaves sound healing, roots music, reggae culture and conscious gatherings into the journey out of the system.

  • Online sound baths and integration circles
  • In-person ceremonies on islands and in nature
  • Reggae and roots nights around fires and on the sand
  • Live acoustic sets at small, long-table suppers
FOOD • FARM • TABLE

Real food, grown somewhere real

The journey out of the system runs through your plate. We connect you with farms, chefs and supper clubs that care about soil, animals, seed, water and the people eating at the table.

  • Farm-to-table suppers with local growers and chefs
  • Workshops on growing, preserving and cooking real food
  • Family-friendly meals that kids actually want to eat
